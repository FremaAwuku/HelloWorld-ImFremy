Zodi-App

Modern Day Social Astrology App

When planning Zodi-App, I asked myself what experience I wanted the user to have. Understanding that most astrology apps dealt with the retrieval of calculated birth dates, I wanted to take my application a step further and introduced a social aspect to it. The entire application exists on 3 main pages , the Sun Sign dealing with calculations, the Zodi-Feed to tackle the interactivity amongst other users, and the User Dashboard that takes care of all user interactions throughout the application.
